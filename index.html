Tony Stark

Genius, Billionaire, Playboy, Philanthropist

Why Tony Stark is my favorite?

  • He is unconventional

    Unlike other superheroes, he doesn't hide his identity. He revealed himself to the press so that the world knows that he is the Iron Man. He does things in his own way, rather than what others expect of him.

  • Outspoken

    Whatever opinion he has of you, he will say right on your face. He doesn't care what you feel about it. If there is something good in you, he will appreciate it. If there is someting bad, he will say right on the spot.

  • Sense of urgency

    He is a mortal being, and the fear of death was the reason that led him to make the Iron Suit. He obsessively upgrades his suit from then, so that he can face the problems that was yet to come.

  • Playfulness

    He is full of fun and a man of high spirits. In his every style, there is a liveliness. He is not boring.

  • He is Mechanic

    He haven't got any supernatural powers. His true power lies in the hardwork that he does alone in his garage. He creates things on his own and keeps himself up to date.
